## Access Muse

### Production (Mainnet)

Use the main application for real impact planning and blockchain attestations:

- **URL**: [https://muse.beaconlabs.io](https://muse.beaconlabs.io)
- **Network**: Ethereum Mainnet
- **Use for**: Production logic models, real Hypercerts, permanent evidence attestations

### Testnet (Development)

Practice and experiment without real blockchain costs:

- **URL**: [https://dev.muse.beaconlabs.io](https://dev.muse.beaconlabs.io)
- **Network**: Ethereum Testnet (Sepolia)
- **Use for**: Learning, testing features, practice minting Hypercerts

:::tip Recommended for First-Time Users
Start with the **Testnet** to explore Muse's features without any costs. Once you're comfortable, switch to Mainnet for your real impact work.
:::

## Quick Start: Your First 5 Minutes

Here's how to create your first evidence-based logic model:

1. **Visit the App** - Go to [muse.beaconlabs.io](https://muse.beaconlabs.io) or the testnet
2. **Navigate to Canvas** - Click on the canvas/logic model builder
3. **Generate with AI** - Click "Generate Logic Model" and describe your impact goal
   - Example: "OSS impact on Ethereum ecosystem"
   - Example: "Reducing food insecurity in urban communities"
4. **Review Results** - In ~40 seconds, see your generated logic model with:
   - Cards representing stages (Activities → Outputs → Outcomes → Impact)
   - Arrows showing causal relationships
   - Green edges indicating evidence-backed connections
5. **Explore Evidence** - Click on green edges to see the peer-reviewed research supporting your logic

That's it! You've created an evidence-based Theory of Change.
